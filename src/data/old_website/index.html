About Me

My name is Christian DerManuelian, and I just recently graduated from UC San Diego with a B.S. in Data Science. My degree focused on the software development side of data analytics. Learning to code in Python and C++, I smoothly transitioned into the world of AI and Machine Learning. My first ever neural network I built was a simple CNN to classify low-resolution images of food. From that moment I knew Machine Learning was the field for me. I went on to get a proper, low-level understanding of Deep Neural Nets, RNN's, Language Models for NLP, and ended off my degree with Reinforcement Learning techniques. I loved working with all of these frameworks, and am itching to work with them in industry. I aspire to acquire experience working with AI and broadening my practical skillest so I may one day do research developing state-of-the-art Machine Learning methods and techniques.
I currently live in Los Angeles and am searching for work in the Southern California area. My time is spent doing coding and robotics projects, as well as online classes in Machine Learning. My free time is spent training at my school's gym, playing goalie in Ice Hockey, playing the piano, and hiking.
